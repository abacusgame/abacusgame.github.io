Drone

One of my dreams is to fly in the sky freely. Now drone is getting popular these days.

They use VR camera to watch the scene from the drone. Instead of VR camera,

I’d like to propose a way to use 360 degrees camera. Not only one screen ahead, but also cameras on both sides, up and down, and behind your body. Then you will feel like you’re really sitting in a cockpit of the drone. You can observe the scenery from the sky like you’re in a helicopter. You can hover above houses, fly around skyscrapers, explore into a deep forest or castles of another countries from your room. It’s a kind of teleportation or magic I guess. Internet transfer information with the speed of light. The time lag will be reduced to a satisfiable degree, so it would become a real experience of teleexistance. Scenery of video games aren’t real. They look similar to the real world, but not actually real. Artificial sceneries are not exciting. But cameras on drones provide the real world, so it would be exciting. It may be immersible experience. 3D video games make me sick. So the real scenery would make me sick too. Even if such technology becomes popular, I would be bored soon. That would become just a boring job like driving a car.

Similarly, air planes can be operated in a same way I guess. This is a positive way of utilizing drone technology. I hate to ride in a real helicopter because it’s dangerous, but drone would be safe. It’s not difficult, but a bit costly.
